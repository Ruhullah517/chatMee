About Us

Symptom Checker is a chat-based informative service that employs cutting-edge technology to model the probability association between risk factors, illnesses, and symptoms based on the responses you offer. At the completion of the evaluation, you will be given some information about the area and timeframe in which your symptoms may require treatment, as well as some information about probable conditions based on the information you supplied. Symptom Checker will attempt to lead you to the appropriate information, but it is not a substitute for consulting a doctor or other healthcare professional if you are ill. You should not take or discontinue any action (such as taking medications) solely on information provided by the service.
